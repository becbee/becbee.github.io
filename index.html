My Top Yarn

I prefer acrylic yarns, and my favourite of those are the Women's Institute Yarns usually available on 3 for 2 at Hobbycraft. They come in a whole range of colours. They can be a little stiff when first workes up, but if you give them a wash with a conditioner they'll soften right up - or they'll just soften through use.
